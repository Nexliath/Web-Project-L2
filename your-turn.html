Protect Lin, the asian warrior
It's your turn, discover the hidden cities

The goal of the game

2000 years ago, the young warrior LIN had the mission under the orders of the Empress of China. His mission was to list all the most magnificent and intriguing places in Asia. With your help, he will have to find the names of the Asian cities that stand before him. Otherwise, he will take the risk of being caught by the bandits, and therefore of being hanged. If your mission is carried out, a surprise will appear

You have at your disposition a keyboard that will help you to find the secret city. To restart, you just have to refresh the page.
